Fixed vs variable

Fixed: Stays the same every month (rent, insurance, loans).
Variable: Changes month to month (food, gas, entertainment). You can often cut variable expenses.

Understanding your options

1
❄️ Snowball strategy

Pay off smallest debts first for quick wins and motivation. Takes longer but feels like progress.

2
🏔️ Avalanche strategy

Pay highest interest first to save the most money overall. Mathematically optimal.

3
💰 Adjust your payment

Drag the slider to model different monthly payment amounts. See the impact instantly.

4
📅 Track milestones

Know exactly which debts get paid at 3, 6, 12, 16 months — feel the progress.

💡 Pro move: Combine the slider with cutting one variable expense. See how a $100 cut + $50 extra payment = debt-free 6 months faster. Small changes compound.
